Our Features

Digital Support

Download our app and find everything you need to know about your custom van. Features detailed guides on solar, electrical, insulation, product guides and more!

Solar Panels

Essential for planning your off-grid lifestyle! We’ve done the calculations on how much electricity you will use on average based on the electrical components of your van.

Built-in Wi-Fi

When you leave one of our mapped-out wifi zones, no need to interrupt your streaming. Our vans have built-in wifi with pay-for-use and unlimited plans.

Survival Kit

We go beyond far bandages and alcohol pads. Our survival kit includes tents made from finest materials that can be set up on the roof of your van. Check your app for a set up guide.
